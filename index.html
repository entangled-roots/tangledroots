Community Map

Rooted are creating a map of communities dedicated to foraging, herbalism, conservation, community gardening, and more—where nature’s wisdom meets grassroots connection. The aim is to help people easily find communities whether moving to a new city, knowing what’s going on in your current area, or visiting new places.

Click on the icons on the map for details, or find the list version below the map. We recommend you check the community's website for the most up to date information about volunteering times and opportunities.
