Community Map

Rooted are creating a map of communities dedicated to foraging, herbalism, permaculture, community gardening, and more—where nature’s wisdom meets grassroots connection. The aim is to help people easily find communities whether moving to a new city, knowing what’s going on in your current area, or visiting new places.

Look for the following icons on the map to find communities:

-

Foraging

Growing

Herbalism

Click on the icons on the map for details, or find the list version below the map.

Add a community

Add a community to Rooted Community Map

We are adding to Rooted Community Map over time as new communities come under our radar. It is evolving! We also welcome you to help us grow the hub by adding communities you are part of to the map directly. To do so, fill in the form below. We know sometimes communities do not have fixed addresses, so we ask you make an approximation using the post code, and ideally a street name too which feels relevant. Your addition will not appear immediately on the map - expect to see it in a few days!.
