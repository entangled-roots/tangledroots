Roots Community Map

We’re creating a map of communities dedicated to foraging, herbalism, permaculture, community gardening, and more—where nature’s wisdom meets grassroots connection. The aim is to help people easily find communities whether moving to a new city, knowing what’s going on in your current area, or visiting new placesl

Add your community

We are adding to Rooted Community Map over time as new communities come under our radar, it is evolving! We also welcome you to add your community to the map directly. To do so, follow these instructions.

  • 1. Click on the plus icon in the top right corner of the map
  • 2. Provide the name of the community
  • 3. For marker, select which category you think captures the community best.
